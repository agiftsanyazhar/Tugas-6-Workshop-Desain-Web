Alerts

Alerts are created with the .alert class, followed by a contextual color classes:

Success! This alert box could indicates a successful or positive action.
Info! This alert box could indicates a neutral informative change or action.
Warning! This alert box could indicates a warning that might need attention.
Danger! This alert box could indicates a dangerous or potentially negative action.
Primary! Indicates an important action.
Secondary! Indicates a slightly less important action.
Dark! Dark grey alert.
Light! Light grey alert.
